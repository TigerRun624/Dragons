Antipodean Opaleye

Found in: Valleys, New Zealand / Austalia

Firebreathing: vivid scarlet flame

Appearance: pearl colored scales, multi-colored pupil-less eyes

Weight: 2-3 tons (medium)

Diet: sheep, sometimes kangaroos

Egg Appearance: pale gray and fossil-like

This information was gathered from Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them by Newt Scamander.
