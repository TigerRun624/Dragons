Chinese Fireball

Other Names: Liondragon

Ablities: mushroom-shaped flame from nostrils

Weight: 2-4 tons (females are larger)

Appearance: scarlet and smoothy striking appearance.-scaled, it has a fringe of golden spikes around its snub-snouted face and extremely protuberant eyes.

Eggs are a vivid crimson speckled with gold and the shells are much prized. The Fireball is aggressive but more tolerant of its own species than most dragons, sometimes consenting to share its territory with up to two others. The Fireball will feast on most mammals, though it prefers pigs and humans.
