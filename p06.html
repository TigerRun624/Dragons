Hungarian Horntail

Found in: Hungary, Europe

Firebreathing: up to fifty feet

Appearance: black scales, lizard-like, bronze horns, bronze tail spikes, yellow eyes

Weight: varies

Diet: goats, sheep, and humans

Egg Appearance: cement-colored, hard-shelled
